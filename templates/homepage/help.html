How does Our Site Work ?

Please find below the Steps to go about the Disease Prediction System.
Click to Learn More


• Registration on the site is either a Patient or a Doctor
• As a Patient, there are three options - check the disease, view consultation history, and give feedback
• You can check the disease by entering the symptoms that you suffer from
• Click on predict button to get the predicted disease and confidence score
• The system suggests specialized doctors for the predicted disease
• Patients can access a list of these doctors, ability to chat with them online and give an evaluation to them
• Also the system allow patients to give feedback and view the consultation history
• As a Doctor, you can view the consultation history with patients, and give feedback to the system
