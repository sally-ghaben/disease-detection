Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the benefits of using a Disease Prediction System?

    • The system can assist healthcare professionals in making more accurate diagnoses, leading to timely and appropriate treatment.
    • It can also help in early detection of diseases, potentially improving patient outcomes.
    • Additionally, the system can reduce the workload on healthcare providers by automating the initial screening process and allowing them to focus on critical cases.
    • Overall, it has the potential to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of healthcare delivery.

  • How does the System ensure Accuracy in disease prediction?

    • The accuracy of the system depends on the quality and quantity of data used for training the machine learning model.
    • It's important to note that while Disease Prediction Systems strive for accuracy, they are not infallible. Clinical judgment and professional evaluation should always be sought for definitive diagnoses and treatment decisions.
    • These systems are meant to assist healthcare professionals and empower patients but should not replace human expertise and comprehensive medical evaluation.

  • How does the Disease Prediction System based on Symptoms work?

    • The Disease Prediction System utilizes machine learning techniques to improve the treatment of patients.
    • When a patient enters their symptoms as input, the system evaluates the symptoms and predicts the disease as an output.
    • The system relies on a dataset of symptoms and diseases, which is used to train a machine learning model. This model learns relationships between symptoms and diseases from the training data.
    • When the symptoms is entered, the model applies its learned knowledge to predict the most likely disease based on the input.
    • Feedback from users, healthcare professionals, and new data can be used to update the system, enhance its accuracy, and incorporate new knowledge into the prediction model.

  • Are Disease Prediction Systems suitable for self-diagnosis at home?

    It is recommended to use Disease Prediction Systems as supportive tools alongside professional medical consultation. If individuals have health concerns, it is advisable to consult with a healthcare professional who can provide a comprehensive evaluation and accurate diagnosis based on a holistic understanding of the individual's health and medical history.

  • How does the system handle the input of Symptoms from user?

    The system provides a list of symptoms, where the users can add whatever symptoms they are having from a drop-down consisting of 132 symptoms listed. Whatever symptoms they chose get added to the system list after which they can click on predict to get their prognosis.
