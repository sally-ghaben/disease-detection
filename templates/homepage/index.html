Cardiology

Diseases seen by a Cardiologist doctor include:
Heart attack, Bronchial Asthma, Hypertension

A cardiologist is a healthcare provider who can treat high blood pressure and heart failure, as well as problems with your heart valves and blood vessels issues. They can treat or help you prevent a number of cardiovascular problems.

Neurology

Diseases seen by a Neurologist doctor include:
Varicose veins, Paralysis (brain hemorrhage), Migraine, Cervical spondylosis

A neurologist is a medical doctor who diagnoses, treats and manages disorders of the brain and nervous system. A neurologist knows the anatomy, function and conditions that affect your nerves and nervous system.

Dermatology

Diseases seen by a Dermatologist doctor include:
Acne, Chicken pox, Fungal infection, Psoriasis, Impetigo

A dermatologist is a medical doctor who specializes in conditions that affect the skin, hair, and nails. Whether it's rashes, wrinkles, psoriasis, or melanoma, no one understands your skin, hair, and nails better than a dermatologist.

Rheumatology

Diseases seen by a Rheumatologist doctor include:
Osteoarthristis, Arthritis

A rheumatologist is a board-certified internist or pediatrician who is qualified by additional training and experience in the diagnosis and treatment of arthritis and other diseases of the joints, muscles, and bones.
